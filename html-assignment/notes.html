Notes

HTML tags:

img
The image tag alows the site to have a image on it and requires 2 different attributes:
  • src
  • alt
a
The a tag alows the site to have a link on it and requires 1 attribute:
  • href
blockquote
This tag is used when you want to add a quote to your site
sub
Used for text formating when site needs to text go down
strong
Used to make text stronger or other words bold
